About Melbourne

As the capital of the state of Victoria, Melbourne is home to a dynamic urban environment that blends historic architecture with modern infrastructure, vibrant public spaces, and a globally connected business and research community. Melbourne is internationally renowned for its strong academic ecosystem and hosts several world-class universities and research institutions, including the prestigious University of Melbourne, which will serve as a host institution for this conference.
